Welcome To West-MEC NEC Coding Program

West-MEC is a public high school here in Arizona! The coding program is full of tons of learning involving HTML, CSS, JavaScript and so much more. This expirenece is once in a lifetime, and could help you decifer what career you want to take on in the future. The program is offered to juniors and seniors as a two-year program with much to explore!


Member Of The Month

The member of the month award is very special to the students here at West-mec. It gives them an opportunity to let their peers and family know how hard they've been working. The member of the month award includes a certificate and a free West-MEC shirt.
