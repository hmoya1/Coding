Information

What is West-MEC?

West-MEC is an opportunity for you to learn more about a field you're interested in. It gives you a chance to work side by side with a professional and with people who are working in the same field as you. In the one-two years you'll attend West-MEC you'll learn everything you need to know and become certified in your specialty!
